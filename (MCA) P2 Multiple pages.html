About Us:

LMCST having 19 years of academic excellence, was established in the year 2002 by the Lourdes Matha Catholic Educational Society and is being managed by the Archdiocese of Changanacherry. The campus is spread over 25 acres at Kuttichal, in the vicinity of the capital city, a scenic location with the picturesque lush greenery in the backdrop of misty Western Ghats, the perfect ambience for academic pursuit. We have excellent infrastructure, smart class rooms, laboratories, hostels, modern conference halls and amenities for co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Well experienced and dedicated faculty imparting quality education has been the hall mark of our college.
